Termingenie - Appointment Genius

A SaaS startup for online appointment booking (2011-2020)

Back in 2011, I started a small company around a product targeting the German-speaking market. The goal was to create a Software as a Service (SaaS) platform to offer online appointment booking for mid-size companies.

"Termingenie" means "appointment genius" in German.

Why It Ended

Due to economic factors and specifically because of too much competition in this product space, I stopped active development around 2017 and closed the service completely in 2020.

The appointment booking market became increasingly crowded with well-funded competitors, making it difficult for a small, bootstrapped company to compete effectively.
